About GEC Kushanagar

Government Engineering College, Kushanagar is a premier institution providing quality education in various fields of engineering. Established with the mission to foster excellence in education, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs to create a strong foundation for students' future careers.

Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.E) in Computer Science Engineering
  • Bachelor of Technology (B.E) in Civil Engineering
  • Bachelor of Technology (B.E) in Electrical Engineering
  • Master of Technology (B.E) in Mechanical Engineering
