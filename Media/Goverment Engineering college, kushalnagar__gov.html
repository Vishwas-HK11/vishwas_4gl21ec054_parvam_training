About college

Government engineering college Kushalnagar ,is the engineering college where thousands of students get knowledge in engineering and technology sector.Government Engineering College, Kushalnagar (GECK), established in 2007, is a government-affiliated institution under Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi. Located in the serene backdrop of Kodagu, Karnataka, the college spans a 10-acre campus in the central part of Kushalnagar
Courses Offered:
GECK offers four Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) programs:

  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
